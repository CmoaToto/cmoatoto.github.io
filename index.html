Leading the Money migration

2026

Rounding bugs on money are exactly the 0.1% that quietly erodes trust. I led a small team (plus two custom AI agents I wrote) to move the whole app onto a type-safe Money type, across ~100 pull requests. No more cents-rounding incidents.

Tech leadRefactoringAI tooling

Killing the last XML screen

2026

I led the team, up to eight engineers, that migrated our last 86 XML screens to Compose across seven teams, in about eight weeks. We'd estimated twenty-nine. The trick: a self-improving orchestrator skill that got a little sharper with every screen it touched.
