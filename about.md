Hi, I'm **Antonin Fouques**, an Android Staff Engineer based in {{ site.location }}.

For more than ten years I've been building Android apps, from accelerometer games on
early Nokia phones to the banking experience that European SMEs and freelancers rely on
every day at [Qonto](https://qonto.com). These days I spend my time leading our move to
**Jetpack Compose**, bringing **AI** into how the Android team works, and mentoring engineers.

Before Qonto I was an Android developer and Scrum Master at **Genymobile** (2013–2018),
where I went deep on the Android platform and AOSP. For over a decade I also helped organize the
**Paris Android User Group**, **Droidcon Paris** and **AndroidMakers**, growing one of
Europe's liveliest Android communities.

I occasionally write on [Medium](https://medium.com/@{{ site.medium_username }}) and ship the
odd side project. The next article on my list: migrating Qonto's last 100+ XML screens to Compose.
