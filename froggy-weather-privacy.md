## 🛡️ Overview

Froggy Weather is a fun, educational weather app designed for children and families. We are committed to protecting your privacy and your child's privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

## 📍 Information We Collect

### Location Information
- **What we collect:** Your device's location (city/region level)
- **Why we need it:** To provide accurate weather information for your area
- **How we use it:** Only to fetch weather data from our weather service
- **Storage:** Location data is not stored permanently on our servers

### Weather Preferences
- **What we collect:** Your favorite locations and weather display preferences
- **Why we need it:** To personalize your weather experience
- **How we use it:** Stored locally on your device only
- **Storage:** Data remains on your device and is not shared

### Technical Information
- **What we collect:** App usage statistics (crashes, performance)
- **Why we need it:** To improve app stability and performance
- **How we use it:** Anonymous data to fix bugs and enhance features
- **Storage:** No personal information is included in technical data

## 🔒 How We Protect Your Information

### Security Measures
- All data transmission uses secure, encrypted connections
- No personal data is stored on external servers
- Location data is processed anonymously
- App data is stored securely on your device

### Third-Party Services
We use OpenWeatherMap for weather data:
- **Data shared:** Only your location (city level)
- **Purpose:** To receive weather information
- **Privacy:** OpenWeatherMap's privacy policy applies to weather data requests

## 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Parental Rights and Controls

### For Parents and Guardians
- **Review:** You can review all app permissions in your device settings
- **Control:** You can revoke location access at any time
- **Delete:** You can delete the app and all data by uninstalling
- **Contact:** Reach us at froggyweather.privacy@gmail.com for questions

### Your Rights
- **Access:** View what data the app has (stored locally on device)
- **Delete:** Remove all app data by uninstalling the app
- **Opt-out:** Disable location services in device settings
- **Questions:** Contact us about any privacy concerns

## 👶 Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

### Special Protections for Children Under 13
- **No Personal Information:** We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
- **Parental Consent:** Location access requires parent/guardian device permission
- **Safe Experience:** No chat features, social sharing, or user-generated content
- **Educational Purpose:** App is designed for learning about weather

### If We Discover Child Data
If we discover we have collected personal information from a child under 13:
- We will delete the information immediately
- We will not use the information for any purpose
- We will strengthen our safeguards to prevent future collection

## 🌍 International Users

App complies with:
- GDPR (European Union)
- PIPEDA requirements (Canada)
- CCPA guidelines (California)
- Global privacy best practices

## 📱 App Permissions Explanation

### Location Permission
- **Required for:** Getting weather for your area
- **Frequency:** Only when you open the app or refresh weather
- **Accuracy:** City-level location (not precise GPS)
- **Control:** Can be disabled in device settings

### Internet Permission
- **Required for:** Downloading weather information
- **Usage:** Only connects to weather services
- **No tracking:** We don't track your browsing or other apps
- **Offline mode:** App works with cached data when offline
